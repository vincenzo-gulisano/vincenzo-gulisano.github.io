I am very happy to announce that our grand challenge solution, <strong>Deterministic Real-Time Analytics of Geospatial Data Streams through ScaleGate Objects</strong>, won the the Best Grand Challenge Solution Award at the Distributed-Event based Systems (DEBS) 2015 conference!!!

You have more details about our work (and the challenge) <a href="http://vincenzogulisano.com/2015/06/18/accepted-paper-debs-2015-grand-challenge/">here</a>.

I would like to thank Yiannis, Ivan, Marina and Philippas for the hard work!!! I would also like to thank Zbigniew Jerzak and Holger Ziekow for setting up such a nice challenge, and the organizers of DEBS 2015 for the great conference. Finally, many thanks to Rocio for the help with the graphics!
